Vehicle SKD Assembly Factory

Our SKD (Semi Knocked Down) assembly operations represent a strategic approach to vehicle manufacturing, combining Chinese automotive expertise with local Malaysian assembly capabilities.

Key Benefits

Reduce Tariff Impact

Localized assembly significantly reduces import costs compared to whole vehicle imports

Save Assembly Costs

Lower labor costs and enhanced cooperation with local Malaysian enterprises

Create Employment

Generate tax revenue and employment opportunities for the local community

Industrial Upgrading

Introduce advanced technology and cultivate technical professionals

Timeline

Phase I

November 2025

Initial setup focusing on urban functional vehicles, sanitation vehicles, and electric heavy-duty truck markets

Phase II

January 2027

Self-built vehicle assembly and testing capabilities with semi-solid battery technology and extended-range heavy-duty trucks
